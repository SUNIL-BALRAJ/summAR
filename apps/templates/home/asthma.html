Exercises

Diaphragmatic Breathing

This exercise focuses on deep breathing, engaging the diaphragm to improve lung capacity and oxygen exchange.

Pursed Lip Breathing

Pursed lip breathing helps regulate breathing, reduce shortness of breath, and increase the efficiency of oxygen exchange.

Walking

Regular walking, especially in fresh air, can help improve cardiovascular health, lung capacity, and overall respiratory function.

Gentle Aerobic Exercises

Low-impact aerobic exercises like stationary cycling and light swimming can strengthen respiratory muscles and improve lung efficiency.

Yoga

Certain yoga poses and breathing techniques can promote lung expansion, increase oxygen flow, and reduce stress, benefiting respiratory health.

Diet Plan

Anti-Inflammatory Foods

Include foods rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties, such as fruits (berries, citrus fruits), vegetables (leafy greens, tomatoes), and nuts.

Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Foods high in omega-3 fatty acids like fatty fish (salmon, tuna) and flaxseeds may help reduce inflammation and support lung health.

Vitamin C-Rich Foods

Citrus fruits, bell peppers, and broccoli are good sources of vitamin C, which can support the immune system and respiratory health.

Fluids

Staying well-hydrated with water and herbal teas can help keep the respiratory passages moist and aid in thinning mucus.
